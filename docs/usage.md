## `/start`

**Dump view (default)**

- Type ideas into the textarea. `Cmd+Enter` or `Ctrl+Enter` to save. Ideas persist across sessions.
- Mic button for voice input.
- Saved ideas appear below the textarea. Hover to delete.

Once you have at least one saved idea, "Run pipeline →" appears in the bottom right. Claude analyzes your ideas, identifies 2–3 markets they point to, and asks you to pick one at the first checkpoint.

**Market view** — "pick a market →" in the bottom right (for when you already know your market)

Four ways to name your market:

- **Browse** — pick from 13 curated categories
- **Type it** — type your market directly (e.g. `freelance video editors`)
- **Competitor** — name a product you want to compete with (e.g. `Notion`)
- **Subreddit** — name the community your user lives in (e.g. `personalfinance` → `r/personalfinance`)

Once a market is set, a bar appears at the bottom. "Run pipeline →" skips market identification entirely and goes straight to research — no Checkpoint 0.
